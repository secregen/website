Email Threats to Your Organizatioin

Phishing and Spamming

  • Malicious emails from attackers, which look like normal business emails, attempt to steal your information or control your computer.

No Authentication, No Confidentiality

  • No authentication or confidentiality for authentic business emails.

Secregen Stops the Threats for You

  • Signature: similar to signing a contract or letter on paper, leave your digital signature on your email to confirm that it is you who send the letter, not someone else.
  • Encryption: make your email secret so that only intended recipients can decrypt and read it.

System Setup

  • Use the cloud service; no setup tasks.
  • Or your administrator sets up the system easily on premises.
  • No end user configuration in either case.
