Future of Email Security

At Secregen, we are revolutionizing enterprise end-to-end email security.

  • Trust your people, not third-party certificate authorities: an enterprise is an autonomous certificate authority by itself and for itself. It certifies its employees and business partners autonomously.
  • New approach to security management: no end-user configuration is needed, and the administrator centrally manages keys, certificates, and trustworthiness.
  • Employees may flexibly define trust boundaries according to their business needs.

In addition, we are also revolutionizing personal end-to-end email security.

  • You have total control of your trusted correspondents with no manual configuration.
