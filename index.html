Revolutionizing Email Security

At Secregen, we are revolutionizing enterprise email security.

  • End-to-end security with the innovative trust model: trust your people, not third-party certificate authorities. An enterprise is the autonomous certificate authority by itself and for itself. It certifies its employees and business partners autonomously.
  • The new approach to security management: the administrator centrally manages keys, certificates, and trustworthiness, and no end-user configuration is needed.
  • Employees may flexibly define trust boundaries according to their business needs.
  • Support auditing encrypted messages without exposing employees' private keys.

We are also revolutionizing personal end-to-end email security.

  • You have complete control of your inbound and outbound email messages.
  • No manual configuration: the single mouse click sets you up.
  • No auditing.
  • Easy to use with intuitive user interfaces.
