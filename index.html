Email Threats to Your Organizatioin

  • Phishing/spamming: malicious emails from attackers, which look like normal business emails, attempt to steal your information or control your computer.
  • Want to show real identity (authentication) and keep confidentiality for authentic business emails.

Secregen Stops the Threats for You

  • Sign: similar to sign a contract or letter, use your digital pen to sign your email to confirm that it is you who send the letter, not someone else.
  • Encrypt: make your email secret so that only intended recipients can decrypt and read it.
